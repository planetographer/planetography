Uranus

Facts:

  • Seventh planet in the solar system
  • One fact: Although Neptune on average is colder than Uranus, the coldest ever recorded actually belongs to Uranus.
  • Diameter: ~ 51118 km
  • Moons: 28 confirmed moons, notable ones are: Titania, Miranda, Umbriel, Ariel, and Oberon
  • Type: Ice giant planet
  • Axial tilt: ~ 98 degrees (Rotates sideways, possibly due to huge impacts long ago).
  • Day: ~ 17 hours
  • Full orbit: 30687 Earth days
  • Average surface temperature: -195Â° Celsius
  • Name Origin (English): Uranus is from Latin for god of the sky. However, this closely resembles the Greek term for the god of the sky "Ouranos".
  • Origin: Formed about 4.5 billion years ago from gas and dust particles.
  • Atmosphere: Composed of 83% hydrogen, 15% helium, and 2% methane.
