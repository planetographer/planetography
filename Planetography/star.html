Star

What is a star?:

  • A star is a celestial object mainly composed of hydrogen and helium.
  • During nuclear fusion, a star generates heat and light.
  • The birth of a star is from gravity contracting gas and dust together until the center is hot enough to generate energy.
  • A star can also end once they burn out their hydrogen, becoming a red giant phase. After that, the star creates a nebula, then become a white dwarf, where they slowly fade away.
